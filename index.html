Our love story

Connor and Taylor's love story starts at the Dutch Brothers on Southern and Greenfield in September of 2022, when Taylor gave Connor a kazoo she made out of a straw and asked him to join a band she made up with their coworkers. What started as coworkers quickly moved to friends, and then came a beautiful relationship, creating the most amazing memories and moments.

Now, in the past 4 years, they've gone on hundreds of dates, bought countless flowers, had way too much caffeine, and shared the things they love most with each other. Through every high and every challenge, they've grown side by side, learning each other more deeply and choosing each other again and again.

Then one August day in 2025, Connor somehow convinced her he had tickets to a concert on the Diamond Rim up near Payson. She had no idea until she saw the lights and sunflowers on the sunset cliffside the life-changing question he was about to ask her. After getting her out of the car, he knelt down and asked her the question. And that's where the new chapter in their lives began.

They are so excited to share this chapter with you— and it starts on October 21st, 2026..
